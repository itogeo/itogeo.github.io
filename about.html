Practical Geospatial Support for Local Projects

Ito Geospatial helps municipalities, watershed groups, and regional agencies make sense of their terrain and imagery.

Our Story

Ito Geospatial is built to support the public sector with work that holds up under review. We bring remote sensing and elevation expertise to teams that don't have an in-house GIS bench, helping them maintain good data, document decisions, and stay ahead of the next floodplain or planning cycle.

The work is intentionally straightforward: gather the best available inputs, clean and align them, and hand back products that real staff can maintain. When clients need field checks, model updates, or training, we stay involved until the data is actually useful on the ground.
